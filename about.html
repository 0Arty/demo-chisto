About us

We’re here to make your home truly clean

Tetiana Aynahoz

CEO and Founder

Chisto Cleaning Services started in Edmonton in 2022 with one simple idea: to clean every home as if it were our own. No rushing, no “good enough” — just professional work with attention to every detail.

Today, we’ve got a full schedule every day, a dedicated team of regular staff, and dozens of happy clients who keep coming back.

We’re not just a cleaning company. We’re a team of people who love order and know how to bring it into your home — with care and respect.

Chisto members
